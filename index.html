My Dog Muffin

September 30, 2021 by Brianna Dixon

My dog Muffin is a pomeranian papillon. He is black with a white belly and white paws.
He was born on February 8, 2021 and has 5 siblings.
All of his siblings are boys. He loves to bite and eat mangoes. He does not like to be in his cage.
He is very loud in the mornings when he wakes up.

My Hamsters Milkshake and Cookie

September 30, 2021 by Brianna Dixon

I have two hamsters. Their names are Milkshake and Cookie.
Milkshake is white and Cookie is brown. They love to run in
their wheels, eat peanuts, corn, lettuce, spinach, grapes
apples and mangoes. They are also nocturnal animals.
